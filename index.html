    About
    Me

    Hi my name is Jeremy. I graduated from George Washington University in 2017. Since I graduated I have worked in public health and government in New Jersey, Cajola, Guatemala, Sacramento, CA and San Diego, CA. I moved to Sacramento in 2018 where I worked in advocacy, development and outreach for the Sacramento Regional Coalition to End Homelessness while also running the Sacramneto Hunger Coalition. At the start of the pandemic, I moved to San Diego to be closer to my grandmother and worked for EDD for two years. My time at EDD helped me realize my love for problem-solving and has taken me on a new path. After completing multiple classes in Python, I am now enrolled in a bootcamp where I am learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript and hope to start working as a junior developer in the near future.
